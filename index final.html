PROJECT 01 — TEAM HEMISPHERE

Monthly Analytics-Ready Dataset for Grameenphone

📍 DSE: GP
Data Sources
  • Daily stock price history (investing.com), resampled to month-end
  • GP annual reports 2020–2024 — revenue, total assets, EPS
  • World Bank API — Bangladesh inflation & GDP growth
Variables Used
  • month_end_price, monthly_stock_return
  • annual_revenue, annual_total_assets, annual_eps
  • bd_inflation_annual_pct, bd_gdp_growth_annual_pct
Alignment Rule
  • Annual firm & macro figures repeated across each month within their reporting year; keyed to month-end stock date
Missing Value & Outlier Checks
✓ 60/60 months, Jan-2020–Dec-2024, no duplicates
✓ Continuous monthly spacing, no gaps
✓ Apr-2020 price gap explained — DSE closure (26 Mar–31 May 2020)
✓ Jan/Apr/May-2020 return NaNs fully documented
✓ Zero missing values in revenue, assets, EPS
✓ No unexplained outliers in monthly return series

PROJECT 02 — TEAM HEMISPHERE

Market Risk & GARCH Volatility Forecast

📍 NYSE: TGT

A grid-search ARIMA mean specification collapsed to a pure random walk — ARIMA(0,0,0) — confirming zero directional edge. A GARCH(1,1) model with a Student's-t error distribution was used to capture fat-tailed volatility clustering instead.

Model Diagnostics
  • ADF test confirms stationarity of log returns (p < 0.01)
  • Excess kurtosis 36.51, skewness −2.19 — fat tails, left skew
  • Ljung-Box p = 0.33 — residuals are white noise
  • Cumulative 5-day exposure window volatility: 4.15%
💡 Volatility clustering was observed during periods of market uncertainty, including a single-day −25% earnings-gap contraction in May 2022.
